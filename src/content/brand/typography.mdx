## Font Usage

### Headings
- H1: Inter Bold (700) - 2.25rem
- H2: Inter Semibold (600) - 1.875rem
- H3: Inter Medium (500) - 1.5rem

### Body Text
- Regular text: Inter Regular (400) - 1rem
- Medium emphasis: Inter Medium (500) - 1rem
- Strong emphasis: Inter Semibold (600) - 1rem
